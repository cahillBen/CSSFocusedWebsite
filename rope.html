Rope Bracelets

Rope bracelets are fun and easy to make even for beginners. With some practice and colourful rope, it can be a gift that lasts for years. Making a bracelet should take less than 20 minutes when you're comfortable with the steps.

Try it Yourself - Cobra Bracelet

Step 1:

We start by tying a Cow Hitch on one of our buckles and feeding the two ends of our rope through the second buckle. The gap between the buckles should be the size you want your bracelets.

Step 2:

Bring the left rope over the centre ropes, this should leave a loop on the left side. Keep the right side on the right rope on top of the left.

Step 3:

Bring the rope that started on the right underneath the centre ropes and up through the loop.

Step 4:

Tighten the bracelet by pulling both ends of the knot and then push the knot upwards to help fit more knots. Repeat steps 2, 3 but make sure to alternate sides every time (eg. your second time doing step 2 will start with the right rope going over the centre ropes).

Finishing Up

To finish off cut any extra rope off. Next use a candle to melt the ends of the rope to the bracelet. Be Careful, melted paracord is as sticky and hot as melted wax!!!
